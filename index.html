Food Focus Cookie Policy

Effective Date: October 25, 2025

1. What Are Cookies and Tracking Technologies?

Cookies and similar tracking technologies are small files stored on your device or used by our app to recognize your device and remember information about your preferences and usage. These technologies help us improve your experience, analyze app usage, and provide personalized features.

Types of tracking technologies we use include:

  • Cookies – small text files stored on your device to track session data and user preferences.
  • Web beacons / SDK tracking – used in analytics services such as Firebase Analytics to understand app usage patterns.

2. How We Use Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and tracking technologies to:

  • Analyze usage trends – to understand how users interact with the app and improve its functionality.
  • Provide personalized content and AI-based recommendations – through OpenAI API.
  • Ensure app functionality and performance – including crash reporting and stability monitoring via Firebase Crashlytics.
  • Support subscription management and payments – via RevenueCat and Apple Pay SDKs.

We do not use cookies or tracking technologies to sell your personal information or for targeted advertising outside of analytics purposes.

3. Managing Your Preferences

You can control how cookies and tracking technologies are used:

  • Mobile app users: You may disable certain analytics or personalized features by contacting us at support@foodfocusapp.com. Please note that disabling some features may limit the functionality of personalized recommendations.
  • Web users (if applicable): You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information about your usage, including:

  • Firebase Analytics – for app usage analytics.
  • Firebase Crashlytics – for crash and performance monitoring.
  • OpenAI API – for AI-based recommendations and content personalization.
  • RevenueCat – for subscription management and purchase processing.

All third-party services comply with applicable privacy laws, including GDPR.

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. All updates will be posted here with a revised “Effective Date.” We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information.
